Qianfan Shen

EECS, PKU @ undergrad.

I’m a senior undergraduate student from School of EECS, Peking University.

Currently, I’m privileged to be supervised by Prof. Shenlong Wang at UIUC. I have also been working as an research intern at the Visual Computing and Learning Lab (VCL), advised by Prof. Wenzheng Chen and Prof. Baoquan Chen.

In general, my interests lie in 3D computer vision and graphics. More specifically, I’m passionate about building a comprehensive World Simulator, a unified digital-twin framework capable of faithfully reconstructing and simulating the physical world, while allowing users to interact within it
